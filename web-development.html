  • Achieved Endorsement at Lambda School

    To be "Lambda School endorsed," students must achieve four goals. First, pass a behavioral interview about our personal philosophy and how that can be utilized effectively in a professional setting. Second, get through a technical interview that covers all the core code concepts we learned. Third, complete three of four problems in a code challenge hosted on HackerRank. Fourth, explain our solutions in a whiteboarding style interview. I wrote more about the endorsement process and what it taught me on LinkedIn.
